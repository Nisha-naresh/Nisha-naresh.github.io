A little about me

Hi! I am Nisha, a graduate student in Business Analytics and AI at Ontario Tech University, based in Whitby, Ontario, Canada. I come from a Computer Science background where I graduated as Gold Medallist at the top of my class at Saveetha School of Engineering.

My research on mobile health technology was recognised with a Best Paper Presentation award at TECH STAR SUMMIT 2024. I genuinely enjoy finding patterns in messy data and turning them into stories that help people make better decisions.

Outside of academics I love photography, enjoy coding personal projects, and am deeply curious about how data shapes the world around us.

I am currently open to opportunities in data analytics, business intelligence and AI-driven roles. Feel free to reach out!
